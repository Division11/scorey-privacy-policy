Privacy Policy for Scorey App

Last Updated: March 25, 2026

Division 11 Studios ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Scorey mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

Scorey does not require user accounts. You can use the App entirely anonymously. If you use our "Contact Us" feature, you may optionally provide your email address via EmailJS. We do not use background analytics.

2. Data Storage & Third Parties

  • Local Storage: Game history is stored locally on your device (SharedPreferences).
  • AdMob: We use Google AdMob for ads. They may use device identifiers to show relevant ads.
  • In-App Purchases: Handled securely by Apple/Google stores.
