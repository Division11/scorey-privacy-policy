Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 25, 2026

Welcome to Scorey. Your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information within our application, developed by Division 11 Studios.

1. Information Collection and Use

  • Personal Data: Scorey does not require user accounts. You can use the App entirely anonymously. We do not collect names, phone numbers, or precise location data.
  • Contact Information: If you use our "Contact Us" feature, you may optionally provide your email address. This is used solely to respond to your inquiries via EmailJS. If no email is provided, the message remains anonymous.
  • No Automated Tracking: We do not use background analytics or tracking software to monitor your usage of the App.

2. Data Storage & Third-Party Services

  • Local Storage: Your game history and score calculations are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. We do not have access to this data.
  • Google AdMob: We show ads using Google AdMob. AdMob may use unique identifiers to serve appropriate advertisements in compliance with store guidelines.
  • In-App Purchases (IAP): All transactions are processed securely by Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not store your financial or credit card information.

3. Sharing and Permissions

The App does not request access to your GPS or location. Any data shared via the "Share" feature is processed entirely on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

4. Children's Privacy

Scorey is intended for users of all ages. We ensure that advertisements comply with appropriate content standards. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it periodically.
