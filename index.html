Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes the policies and procedures of Division 11 Studios ("we," "us," or "our") regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use the Scorey mobile application (the "Service").

1. Introduction

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through compliance with this policy. This Service is designed to function as an anonymous game score calculator. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Types of Data Collected

A. Personal Data

While using our Service, we do not require you to provide any personally identifiable information (PII) to access core functionalities. However, if you choose to utilize the "Contact Us" feature, you may voluntarily provide your email address. This information is used exclusively for support-related correspondence.

B. Usage and Device Data

The Service may utilize third-party SDKs, specifically Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, and Unity LevelPlay (for ad mediation), which may collect certain non-personally identifiable information automatically, including but not limited to:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., IDFA for iOS, AAID for Android, or Firebase Installation IDs).
  • Basic device technical information (OS version, device model, and crash logs).
  • Product interaction data (screen views, app launches, and game type usage).
  • Advertising interaction data.

3. Data Storage and Retention

Local Storage & Cloud Syncing: Your primary user-generated content, such as saved game history, is stored locally on your device. However, when utilizing "Spectator Mode," active session data (including scores, game rules, and display names) is temporarily hosted via Firebase Realtime Database to allow remote devices to sync.

Retention: This cloud-hosted spectator data is volatile and is permanently purged from our databases immediately upon the conclusion of the game session. Personal information provided via support emails (EmailJS) is retained only for the duration necessary to resolve the inquiry or as required by applicable legal obligations.

4. Disclosure of Data

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our Service, such as:

  • Google LLC: For app analytics, crash reporting (Firebase Crashlytics), and temporary real-time database hosting.
  • Unity Technologies & Ad Partners: For the provision of advertisement mediation via Unity LevelPlay, which includes various third-party bidding networks.
  • EmailJS: For the secure transmission of support requests.
  • App Store Providers: Apple Inc. and Google LLC for the processing of In-App Purchases (IAP).

5. Security of Data

The security of your data is important to us. We employ industry-standard encryption protocols (TLS/SSL) for data in transit through our third-party service providers. However, please be advised that no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Data Subject Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete the information we have on you. Since your primary data is stored locally, you may exercise these rights by:

  • Deleting specific game entries within the App interface.
  • Clearing the App's cache/data via device settings.
  • Uninstalling the App from your mobile device.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. If we become aware that we have collected Personal Data from a child without verification of parental consent, we take steps to remove that information from our records.

8. Contact Information

For questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact our support team at:

Email: support@division11studios.com

ب. بيانات الاستخدام والجهاز

قد تستخدم الخدمة حزم تطوير برمجيات (SDKs) لجهات خارجية، وتحديداً Firebase Analytics، و Firebase Crashlytics، و Unity LevelPlay (لوساطة الإعلانات)، والتي قد تجمع بعض المعلومات غير الشخصية تلقائياً، بما في ذلك على سبيل المثال لا الحصر:

  • بيانات التفاعل مع المنتج (مشاهدات الشاشة، عمليات تشغيل التطبيق، واستخدام نوع اللعبة).
  • بيانات التفاعل مع الإعلانات.

3. تخزين البيانات والاحتفاظ بها

التخزين المحلي والمزامنة السحابية: يتم تخزين المحتوى الأساسي الذي ينشئه المستخدم، مثل سجل الألعاب المحفوظة، محلياً على جهازك. ومع ذلك، عند استخدام "وضع المتفرج" (Spectator Mode)، يتم استضافة بيانات الجلسة النشطة (بما في ذلك النتائج، وقواعد اللعبة، وأسماء العرض) مؤقتاً عبر Firebase Realtime Database للسماح بمزامنة الأجهزة عن بُعد.

الاحتفاظ بالبيانات: بيانات المتفرج المستضافة سحابياً هي بيانات مؤقتة ويتم مسحها نهائياً من قواعد بياناتنا فور انتهاء جلسة اللعبة. يتم الاحتفاظ بالمعلومات الشخصية المقدمة عبر رسائل البريد الإلكتروني للدعم (EmailJS) فقط للمدة اللازمة لحل الاستفسار أو كما تقتضي الالتزامات القانونية المعمول بها.
