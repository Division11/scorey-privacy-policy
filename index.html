Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 25, 2026

Welcome to Scorey. Developed by Division 11 Studios, Scorey is a game score calculator designed to respect your privacy. This policy outlines our data practices.

1. Information We Collect

  • Anonymity: Scorey does not require account creation. You use the app anonymously.
  • Optional Contact Data: If you use the "Contact Us" feature, you may choose to provide your email address. This is processed via EmailJS solely for support purposes.
  • Device Identifiers: To serve ads, we may access your device's Advertising ID (IDFA on iOS, AAID on Android). These are handled by Google AdMob according to their policies.

2. Data Storage & Security

  • Local Storage: Your game history is stored exclusively on your device (SharedPreferences). We have no access to this data.
  • Security: All communication via EmailJS is encrypted in transit using industry-standard TLS/SSL protocols.

3. User Rights & Data Deletion

Since your data is stored locally, you have full control over it. You may delete your game history at any time by:

  • Clearing the App Data in your device settings.
  • Deleting the App from your device.
If you have sent us an email and wish for it to be deleted from our support inbox, please contact us at the email below.

4. Third-Party Services

We partner with the following services to provide app functionality:

  • Google AdMob: For advertising and monetization.
  • EmailJS: For support communication.
  • App Stores: Apple and Google securely handle all In-App Purchases (IAP).

5. Children's Privacy

Scorey is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. Ads served within the App are filtered for appropriate content.
